ABOUT

Esse - actual being - essential nature - essence.

Esse does not try to replace any other social media platforms but instead compliment them.

Esse is an educational project still under development. Designed for sharing treasured images in albums that are easily accessed for continual browsing by family and friends and offers safe storage in the cloud of all user assets using Cloudinary.

Cloudinary utilises widely accepted security standards and regulations, enterprise security features and contingency backup processes.
