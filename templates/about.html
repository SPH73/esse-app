ABOUT

Esse - actual being - essential nature - essence.

Esse does not try to replace any other social media platforms but instead compliment them.

The impact of COVID has prevented social interaction for the greater part and we are missing out on memories with loved ones. More than ever there is a need for family albums to preseve and share our memories with those closest to us and that is what Esse is for.

Developed for sharing treasured images in albums that are easily accessed for continual browsing by family and friends and providing safe storage in the cloud of all user assets using Cloudinary.

Cloudinary utilises widely accepted security standards and regulations, enterprise security features and contingency backup processes.

Disclaimer: Esse is an educational project still under development with many additional features to follow and on going impovemnts to the existing ones.
